DFT investigation of **graphene-TiO₂ heterostructures** for
photocatalytic applications. Studied band alignment, charge
transfer, and interface properties.

### Methods

- **Quantum ESPRESSO** — PBE PAW pseudopotentials
- Heterostructure supercell construction
- Band alignment at the graphene-TiO₂ interface
- Charge density difference analysis

### Relevance

Graphene-TiO₂ composites are promising photocatalysts for water
splitting and pollutant degradation. Understanding the interface
at the electronic level guides experimental design.
