DFT analysis of **formamidinium tin iodide (FASI)** — a
lead-free perovskite candidate for photovoltaic applications.
PDOS calculations to understand orbital contributions near
the band edges.

### Methods

- **Quantum ESPRESSO** — PBE PAW pseudopotentials
- Projected density of states (PDOS) for Sn, I, C, N, H atoms
- Band gap estimation and orbital character analysis

### Key findings

- PDOS reveals dominant Sn-5$p$ and I-5$p$ character at band edges
- Defect tolerance analysis suggests potential for efficient
  lead-free photovoltaics
